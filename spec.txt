punctuation and suprasegmentals
----------------------------------------------------
,         multigraph separator
.         syllable separator
1 2       primary and secondary stress markers
.:. .::.  minor and major groups
:         long
,,        half-long
..        ejective
(-+)      raised (diacritic as its own letter)
(+-)      lowered (diacritic as its own letter)
_X (XXX)  delimited diacritics (here X) on prev char
-         literal hyphen (for glosses)
__        literal underscore (for sound change notation)
#         literal hash (for sound change notation)
\X        literal X (where X is any character)

Bare diacritics auto-combine onto the previous character. Delimited diacritics
need to either be preceded by _ or to be enclosed in (). In the latter case,
any number of delimited diacritics may be enclosed.

When compiled to IPA, the tone diacritics combine into the appropriate IPA
multi-tone diacritics (circumflexes, carons, double accents, etc).
